
## Polymarket Trading Bot Research

**Date:** 2026-02-04

**Goal:** Develop a profitable Polymarket trading bot with $100-200 USDC starting capital.

### Key Findings:

*   **API Access:** Polymarket API available for market data, order placement, and builder integration. Docs at docs.polymarket.com.
*   **Market Types:** Short-term (15-min) crypto markets + longer contracts.
*   **Order Placement:** CLOB, accessible via API.
*   **Wallet:** USDC on Polygon.
*   **Latency:** Crucial for arb strategies, dynamic fees in place to limit arb.
*   **Market Making:** Potential opportunity for bots to maintain positions.
*   **Whale Tracking:** Copy-trading possible, track successful wallets.
*   **Risk:** Order book imbalance, cross-market inefficiency.
*   **VPS:** Hetzner VPS possible.
*   **Builder Program:** Grants and rewards available.
