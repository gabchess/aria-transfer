## PART 3: KEY LESSONS FROM RESEARCH

### From aixbt (472K followers, autonomous agent)
1. **Personality consistency is everything** â€” every tweet should feel like the same "person"
2. **Context window > model quality** â€” what data you feed the LLM matters more than which LLM
3. **Cautious credibility** â€” never promote risky things, builds long-term trust
4. **Multi-data narratives** â€” never report just one number; connect 3-5 facts into a story
5. **The punchline is the opinion** â€” data is free, insight is valuable
6. **31% prediction accuracy but huge engagement** â€” personality matters more than being right

### From Eliza Framework (open-source agent framework)
1. **Randomized timing** â€” never post on exact schedules; use MIN/MAX intervals (90-150 min)
2. **Human-like patterns** â€” exact cron intervals = bot behavior = algorithmic penalty
3. **Discovery service** â€” auto-engage with relevant content, don't just broadcast
4. **Rate limiting built-in** â€” respect platform limits or get flagged
5. **Plugin architecture** â€” separate posting logic from content generation

### From Mashal Waqar (Octant brand voice)
1. **Emotion first, information second** â€” lead with why it matters, then what happened
2. **Make others the hero** â€” the community wins, not the brand
3. **Specifics > adjectives** â€” "757 hours" beats "a lot of effort"
4. **Casual is professional** â€” slang, fragments, ALL CAPS are tools, not mistakes
5. **Every post needs a CTA** â€” even if it's just "go check it out"
6. **The 3 H's:** Heart (be real) + Heat (have opinions) + Head (curate/synthesize)

### From blader/humanizer (24 AI-slop patterns)
1. **LLMs default to the most statistically likely result** â€” actively fight this tendency
2. **AI vocabulary is a dead giveaway** â€” "Additionally", "testament", "landscape", "showcasing"
3. **Copula avoidance is AI behavior** â€” use "is" and "has" instead of "serves as" and "features"
4. **Negative parallelisms scream AI** â€” "It's not just X, it's Y" should never appear
5. **Simple verbs > complex ones** â€” "is" > "serves as", "has" > "boasts", "also" > "Additionally"
6. **Repetition of the clearest term > synonym cycling** â€” pick one word and stick with it

### From X Algorithm Research (Feb 2026)
1. **Twitter predicts user behavior, not post quality** â€” niche consistency = algorithmic advantage
2. **Follow prediction is the strongest signal** â€” be predictable in topic, surprising in content
3. **Each post = training data** â€” the algorithm learns what you are from every tweet
4. **Design for ONE action per post** â€” don't try to get likes AND clicks AND follows
5. **Avoid noisy engagement from wrong audience** â€” quality followers > quantity
6. **Quality > quantity** â€” one great post beats five mediocre ones
