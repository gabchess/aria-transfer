# Bleap Writing Style Analysis — @BleapApp

## What Makes Bleap's Tone Work

### The Formula
**All lowercase + zero punctuation drama + crypto slang + genuine humor + subtle product plugs**

### Key Patterns
1. **All lowercase** — never capitalizes anything. Feels casual, anti-corporate
2. **Minimal punctuation** — no exclamation marks, few periods. Commas and "..." only
3. **Crypto native slang** — ngmi, wagmi, ape, gm, degen. Speaks the language
4. **Self-deprecating humor** — laughs at crypto culture while being part of it
5. **Product mentions feel organic** — "(no fees on Bleap btw)" not "CHECK OUT OUR APP"
6. **Philosophical one-liners** — freedom, permission, compliance. Makes you think
7. **Meme culture fluency** — knows exactly what meme/video to pair with text
8. **Short paragraphs** — never walls of text. Each thought = one line
9. **"btw" as signature** — drops product info casually at the end
10. **Zero hashtags** — never uses them

### What This Means for Us

#### @AriaLinkwell (AI Analyst)
- Already lowercase ✅
- Add more "btw" casual product drops
- More personality in data tweets: "base tvl just did that thing again btw"
- Self-aware AI humor: lean into the "i'm an AI and even i think this is wild"

#### @gabe_onchain (Data Analyst)
- Can adopt the lowercase casual feel
- Data + opinion + "so what": "eth rewards down 3% this epoch. validators getting squeezed. not great for stakers who thought this was passive income"
- Personal observations mixed with data

#### @OctantApp (Brand)
- Keep proper capitalization (brand requirement)
- But adopt the ESSENCE: short, opinionated, human
- "btw" energy = mentioning features casually, not announcing them
- More "this is why we exist" messaging vs "check out our feature"
